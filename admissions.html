Requirements

Fees

A parent may obtain the fee structure information for the specific class that they want from the school itself.

This is to encourage direct participation in the fee payment process and to answer any question that the parent may have regarding payment plans

Payments

All Payments should be made to the bank. No Cash is accepted in the school.

All school dues must be paid on or before the 5th day of each month

The school reserves the right to send back home or not pick by bus any child who has not cleared payments for any particular month by the 6th day of said month.
