About Us

General Information.

Boonhouse Pre-School is situated in Ongata Rongai about 500 metres along Gataka road off Magadi Road. It was established to partner with parents in providing a holistic education to their children.

We offer Baby Class, Nursery and Pre-Unit classes for various age groups and stages of child develpment. Our starter curriculum ensures that your child grows up in a steadfast environment and has a sturdy academic foundation.

BoonHouse seeks to stimulate interests and develop skills in pupils through a range of extra curriculum activities. For Example Scouting, Sports & School Trips

Mission

Our Mission is to provide quality education a biblical based learning environment in which students are :

  • Moulded to exploit their full potential both academically and in extra curriculum activities
  • Encouraged to aqcuire values that will enable them to be responsible, upright members of the society

Vision

To produce young boys and girls who are mature mentally, physically, socially and spiritually. i.e :

  • Academically Excellent
  • Gentle mannered
  • High sense of moral uprightness to be carried into their future

Partnership with Parents

The school strongly advocates in the irreplaceable role of the parents in the education of their children. The home is the most vital environment childhood growth and adjustment to life.

It shapes and moulds attitude that will be carried throughout their life. Parents will take part in their education of their children not just financially but by moulding their values and skills and also acting as the first role models to their children. Parents can expect :

  • An open ear concerning the progress of children's education
  • Regular reports and consultations
  • A rapid response to issues and concerns where possible
  • An opportunity to provide encouragement to stimulates further growth and development of the education offered in the school.

Our Motto

Determined To Excell

Living out this motto includes :

  • A commitment to excellence by teachers and pupils in all BoonHouse Educational programmes.
  • A constant challenge to each student to achieve his or her utmost potential in academics and other areas
  • A concerned effort by all associated with BoonHouse to achieve the highest standards of educational excellence by being good stewards of their time
  • To embrace out mission and vision through Christ (Phillipians 4:13)
